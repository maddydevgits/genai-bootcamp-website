Day1 starts with formal orientation about the Cohort, and one-on-one introduction about each other in the cohort. Introduction to GenAI will be introduced and benefits of this will be shared with live examples.

  • Introduction to Cohort.
  • Pre-Requisites and Tools
  • Talk about GenAI.
  • Program Highlights

Today, we kick off our journey into the world of chatbot development with an in-depth exploration of Botpress. As a powerful chatbot development platform, Botpress offers a wide range of features and functionalities to streamline the process of building intelligent conversational agents. Additionally, we'll delve into the exciting realm of Generative AI and its pivotal role in revolutionizing chatbot interactions.

  • Introduction to Botpress.
  • Installation and setup of Botpress on local machines or cloud-based environments.
  • Overview of Botpress architecture and components.
  • Basic Bot Example

Today, we embark on a fascinating journey into the world of Generative AI models and their application in chatbot development. Through interactive discussions and hands-on activities, we'll unravel the intricacies of models like GPT and explore their potential to enhance chatbot conversations.

  • Exploring Botpress.
  • Hands-on session to create simple rule-based chatbots using Botpress.
  • Integration of basic conversational flows and responses.
  • Develop Bot powered through Rule-based.

In today's session, we roll up our sleeves and dive into the practical aspects of chatbot development with Botpress. Through hands-on exercises and guided tutorials, we'll learn how to create simple rule-based chatbots from scratch. From defining conversational flows to integrating basic responses, we'll take our first steps towards building intelligent chatbots that can engage users in meaningful interactions.

  • Exploring GenerativeAI Models.
  • Connecting GPT Model.
  • Fine-tuning and customising GPT.
  • Develop Bot powered by GPT.

Today, we elevate our chatbot development journey by integrating cutting-edge Generative AI models into our Botpress environment. By incorporating pre-trained models like GPT, we'll unlock new possibilities for generating dynamic and contextually relevant responses in our chatbots. Through hands-on exploration and experimentation, we'll learn how to configure and customize Generative AI-powered chatbot responses, empowering us to create more intelligent and responsive conversational agents.

  • Bot for Agriculture.
  • Bot for Healthcare.
  • Exporting as Banner Bots, Websites.
  • Project Competition.
